myth of the moon rabbit

the jade emperor was seeking to find an animal worthy enough to create elixirs for the immortal gods.

he disguised himself as a beggar and cried for help, asking for food. the animals who heard the plea were the fox, the monkey, and the rabbit. the animals searched all over the land and returned with their findings.

the monkey gathered all the fruits in the bushes and trees.

the fox caught all the fish in the sea.

the rabbit, who had looked everywhere to no avail, decided to sacrafice itself into the fire as food.

before the rabbit could jump, the jade emperor revealed himself in his true form and declared the rabbit the most noble.

he took the rabbit with him into the sky and crowned the rabbit with the title of elixir creator.

legend has it that on a full moon, you can see the rabbit pounding away at the mortar and pestle, making elixirs for the gods all night long.
